So I Fixed It

It took me two years, chipping away at this idea. It's about questioning defaults and poking at the unquestioned standards that exist. It's not perfect, but something is 100% more than nothing.

It's not easy to see how much questioning went into this project, but that's what design is, anyway. It's just invisible questioning and confusion and arguing (well, this was just with my PM-self) and a fraction of time actually sitting down and making a line or two.
